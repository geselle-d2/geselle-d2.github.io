The Sound of Greatness

Interesting Facts about Bathory

  • Influenced and defined the sound of Black Metal
  • When everything in Heavy Metal was about getting faster and more aggressive, Bathory did the opposite and released the iconic Hammerheart album.
  • Bathory was basically a one-man show, Quorthon the only regular member. Later the only member.
  • Yes, the guy spitting fire is him
  • At some point Quorthon recorded all instruments by himself!
  • Quorthon died in june 2004, due to an inborn heart disease
  • This was probably the reason Quorthon only toured during the early years of Bathory
  • His true identity was practically unknown until after his death!
  • To this day his songs are occasionally covered by bands such as: Ereb Altor, Watain, Emperor and many more!
  • Some early songs were recorded in a garage or the bathroom to emphasize the low production quality
  • He even released some rock albums under his pseudonym Quorthon
  • In 2021 the band was elected into the Swedish Music Hall Of Fame
